Our Services

We are here to assist you with the following:

Grant Application Assistance

Guidance and support to help you complete and submit grant applications to appropriate agencies.

Resume Writing Help

We offer advice and templates to help you create a professional resume that highlights your skills and experience.

Job Application Support

Assistance with filling out job applications and preparing for interviews to improve your chances of success.

Recommendation Letters

If appropriate, the councillor’s office can provide recommendation letters to support your employment or educational goals.

Community Outreach & Recruitment

We actively recruit motivated community members to join our outreach team. Help us canvass the area, engage with residents, and bring community concerns directly to the councillor’s office.

Other Community Support

We help connect you with other local programs and resources to improve your well-being.
