About the Councilor's Office

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To serve the residents of Success/Trou Macaque with dedication by responding to community concerns efficiently and transparently.

Vision: To foster a safe, clean, and empowered community where every resident's voice is heard and acted upon.

Previously Completed Projects

Community Cleanup Drive

March 2024 – Removed over 2 tons of waste and beautified public spaces in Beetham Gardens such as the Lilda Brown Park, where the corporation would've provided swings, slides and more as well as planting many flowers.

Street Light Installation

January 2024 – Installed 15 new LED street lights in high-risk areas to improve public safety.

Drainage Repair Initiative

November 2023 – Repaired and cleared major drains in Zone A to prevent flooding.
